Blotbot Advertisement

The Blotbot is a revolutionary new service robot that guides visually impaired people throughout their everyday lives. With four high definition cameras and a state-of-the-art sound system, the Blotbot is sure to be one of the most advanced guide robots on the market. With the Blotbot, disabled people will no longer be set apart or be forced to live their lives differently because of their lack of sight. They will finally have all the tools they need to be able to live a normal life.
